Daybreak

75 days. One sunrise at a time.

Daybreak is a 75-day habit tracker built around the rhythms most people actually keep. Pick 75 Soft, 75 Medium, or 75 Hard — or build your own challenge from scratch. Walk it alone, or invite up to five friends to walk alongside you.

No accounts. No ads. No analytics. Your data syncs through your private iCloud and stays with you.

Common questions

Where does my data live?
On your device. If you're signed into iCloud, Daybreak syncs across your devices via your private iCloud container — Apple manages this; Daybreak has no access to it.

What's in Daybreak Pro?
Custom color themes, advanced analytics with weekday and category breakdowns, photo journaling with captions, sunrise share cards, and the buddy feature for walking the challenge with friends. One-time $4.99 purchase — no subscription.

How do buddies work?
Both you and your buddy need iCloud and Daybreak Pro. You send an invite link via the system share sheet; once they accept, you can each see the other's day count, streak, and today's completion percentage. Photos, reflections, and the amounts you log stay private. You can opt in to sharing individual task names but never the values behind them.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?
Open Settings → Daybreak Pro → Restore Purchases. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your Pro entitlement will reactivate.

I missed a day. Can I log it retroactively?
Yes. From the Stats tab, tap any past day on the calendar — you can mark tasks complete or log progress for that day. Days you edit retroactively are marked with a small dot on the calendar so you can tell them apart from days you logged in the moment.

How do I get my photos out of Daybreak?
At challenge completion, the ceremony screen offers a "Save photos to your library" button — this dumps every progress photo to your iOS Photos app via the system share sheet. You can also save individual photos any time from the photo detail view.

I want to delete everything.
Settings → Developer → Wipe all data clears the local store. To also remove the iCloud copy, go to iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Daybreak.
