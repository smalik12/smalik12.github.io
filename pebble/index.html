Pebble

The budget app built around your paychecks.

Most budgeting apps lump your whole month into one big number. Pebble splits everything by paycheck so you always know exactly what's available between now and your next deposit.

No subscriptions. No ads. No bank login. Your data stays on your device.

Common questions

Where does my data live?
On your device. If you're signed into iCloud, Pebble syncs across your devices via your private iCloud container — Apple manages this; Pebble has no access to it.

What's in Pebble Pro?
Unlimited cards and recurring expenses, full transaction history with year recap, multi-person splits, and CSV/PDF export. One-time $9.99 purchase — no subscription.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?
Open Settings → Pebble Pro → Restore Purchase. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your Pro entitlement will reactivate.

Does Pebble connect to my bank?
No. All entries are manual. This is intentional — your financial data never leaves your device.
